Resources & Support

Connecting communities to trusted organizations, advocacy, reporting tools, and support systems.

Crisis Support

Immediate help for mental health crises and urgent emotional support.

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline 24/7 crisis support across the United States.
  • Crisis Text Line Free text-based support for people in crisis.

Sexual Violence Support

Survivor-centered organizations offering support, information, and healing resources.

  • RAINN The largest anti-sexual violence organization in the U.S.
  • SAKI Survivor Resources Information and support related to survivors and sexual assault kit reform.

Missing Persons Help

Tools and organizations focused on reporting, awareness, and family support.

  • NCMEC National Center for Missing & Exploited Children.
  • Black and Missing Foundation Raises awareness for missing persons in marginalized communities.
  • DOJ Missing Persons Action Center Federal reporting and identification guidance.

Provides case support, prevention tools, and public awareness resources for missing children.

Black and Missing Foundation

Co-founded by Derrica and Natalie Wilson to bring attention and resources to missing persons cases involving communities of color.

Support for missing individuals and the families searching for them, including helplines and guidance.

Legal Aid & Advocacy

Justice-focused organizations working against wrongful convictions, racial inequity, and systemic harm.

Innocence Project

Founded by Barry Scheck and Peter Neufeld, this organization works to exonerate wrongfully convicted people through DNA testing and reform.

Equal Justice Initiative

Founded by Bryan Stevenson, EJI challenges racial injustice, excessive punishment, and inequality within the criminal legal system.

International Justice Mission

A global organization working to combat violence, trafficking, and exploitation through legal and survivor-centered interventions.

Annie E. Casey Foundation – Juvenile Justice Advocacy

A national initiative focused on reforming the juvenile justice system, reducing youth incarceration, and promoting community-based alternatives for young people.

Mental Health & Crisis

Culturally relevant support, wellness resources, and mental health access.

The Boris Lawrence Henson Foundation

Founded by Taraji P. Henson to address stigma and expand mental health support in Black communities.

Therapy for Black Girls

Founded by Dr. Joy Harden Bradford to connect Black women and girls with culturally competent mental health resources.

BEAM

Black Emotional and Mental Health Collective offers education, healing tools, and community-based mental health support.

Mental Health Coalition

Provides accessible tools and community-centered resources, including Black mental health support guides.

Community-Led Support

Organizations shaped by survivors, families, advocates, and directly impacted communities.

Wear Orange

Inspired by Hadiya Pendleton, this movement was created by her friends and family to honor her life and raise awareness about gun violence prevention.

HEAR Foundation

A community-centered foundation Founded by Leon Ford Jr., a police shooting survivor, to promote healing, justice, and community transformation.

Safe Kids Worldwide – Walk This Way

A safety awareness initiative by Safe Kids Worldwide focused on preventing pedestrian injuries and promoting safer walking behaviors for children and communities.

Color Of Change

A racial justice organization mobilizing Black communities to challenge inequity and create systemic change.

Federal data and reports on crime, incarceration, victims, and justice system trends in the U.S.

Center for Policing Equity

A research and data-driven organization that analyzes policing practices to identify racial disparities and improve public safety systems.

The Sentencing Project

Research and advocacy organization focused on incarceration, racial disparities, and criminal justice reform.

FBI Crime Data Explorer

Public-facing crime data tool for viewing national law enforcement statistics and trends.

UNODC

Global data, reports, and research from the United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime.
